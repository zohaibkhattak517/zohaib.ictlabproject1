All Book Details

The Alchemist

Author: Paulo Coelho

Rating: 4.5/5

Description: A philosophical book by Paulo Coelho about a young Andalusian shepherd named Santiago who dreams of finding treasure. The story teaches the importance of following one's dreams and listening to one's heart.

To Kill a Mockingbird

Author: Harper Lee

Rating: 5/5

Description: A classic novel by Harper Lee that explores the serious issues of racial inequality and the loss of innocence in the small town of Maycomb, Alabama, during the 1930s.

1984

Author: George Orwell

Rating: 4.7/5

Description: A dystopian novel about a totalitarian regime that controls every aspect of life. The story follows Winston Smith, a low-ranking member of the Party who seeks to rebel against the system.

Pride and Prejudice

Author: Jane Austen

Rating: 4.6/5

Description: A romantic novel that explores the issues of marriage, money, and love. The story follows Elizabeth Bennet, a smart and independent woman, and her complicated relationship with the proud Mr. Darcy.

Moby Dick

Author: Herman Melville

Rating: 4.3/5

Description: A tale of obsession, revenge, and the sea. The story follows Ishmael, a sailor on a whaling ship, and Captain Ahab's single-minded pursuit of the white whale, Moby Dick.
